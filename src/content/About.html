About

Sysmo was originally a basic monitoring tool intended to be a proof of concept for the asynchronous application framework Supercast. Impressed by the uniques properties and promising possibilities of the user interface, our partner at the Regional Council of Aquitaine have found interesting to make a commercial product of this demo. One year and an half of development later Sysmo 1.0 was released. With new original concepts and a clean design, Sysmo is now a scalable, full featured monitoring tool offering EMS functionalities with a latest edge user interface.
